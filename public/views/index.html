About

Hello, I'm Jim, a Front-End Developer. I have an eye for detail and a passion for what I do. I am eager to keep growing and learning as a Developer in this ever-changing field.

I graduated from Prime Digital Academy as a Full-Stack Software Engineer and a Chemistry degree from the University of Minnesota.

I found Chemistry and coding to be very similar. Taking known chemicals and mixing them to achieve a known product is like writing lines of code to make a wanted function. Working in the Chemistry field taught me how to work in a team to achieve project deadlines with high-quality work. It also taught me how to follow guidelines and regulations to complete projects for clients with different needs.

I’ve had great opportunities to grow and develop as a Web Developer and I'm seeking an innovative, forward thinking company that will allow me to continue to do so. I’ve found software development to be rewarding and exciting, while also challenging!

I also enjoy gaming!
